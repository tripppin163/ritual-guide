Ritual Art & Siggy

A simple guide to Siggy and how you can create your own Ritual‑themed art.

Join Creative Community

Who is Siggy?

All you need to know is: Siggy is a black cat with the Ritual logo. Beyond that, let your imagination run wild! XD

Check examples in the #contributions channel and be sure to share your own art there.

Create your own Siggy

Start with a black cat

Any style works: cute, realistic, anime, pixel art — the main thing is that it clearly looks like a black cat.

Add the Ritual logo

Place the Ritual symbol somewhere on Siggy.

Play with themes

AI, blockchain, space, magic, glitches — anything you like, as long as the piece is made with care and heart.

Share in contributions

Post your art in the #contributions channel on Discord so the community can see and support you.

Post on X

Share your work on X and tag @ritualfnd .

Submit your work

Drop it in the Ritual Foundation space on Cura and upload your link to the Quest Page in Ritual Quest.
