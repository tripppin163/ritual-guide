Ritual Assets

Key places where you can be active, contribute, and grow together with the Ritual community.

Active

Creating posts and content

Your posts should be high-quality and original. We welcome content created with care, creativity, and attention to detail. Posts that are clearly generated by AI in a few seconds without any real improvement or personal touch will be treated as spam and may be removed. Please respect the time and attention of the community.

Chat participation

The chat is a space for meaningful, live conversation. Mindless spam, flooding, and one-word messages are not encouraged. We value:

  • Discussing any topics in a respectful way, from everyday life to professional themes (nodes, tech, art).
  • Helping newcomers by answering questions and sharing your experience.
  • Supporting others: reacting to their work on X (Twitter), giving feedback, and sharing advice.
  • Creating helpful resources, such as short guides that answer common questions from newcomers.

Moderators actively monitor what happens in chat. Thoughtful, constructive activity will not go unnoticed. Contributing in a smart and respectful way is the best way to make a name for yourself and become a valuable member of the community.

Remember: our goal is to build a strong, friendly, and growing community where every meaningful contribution matters. Let’s build it together.

Be active on X and Cura

Be active on X and Cura: post content, create art (for ideas check the updates channel), join Discord chats in the community section, help newcomers — and success will be inevitable, buddy!

Also, when you post on X, don’t forget to tag @ritualfnd. If you want more visibility for your work, you can kindly ask people in the Discord chat to like, repost, and support your content.
