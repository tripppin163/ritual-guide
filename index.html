Quick Ritual Guide

Each role embodies a unique part of our cosmic ecosystem. Read on to find clarity, purpose, and instructions for your journey into Ritual.

Why don’t I have a role?

Roles in the Ritual community are given to those who prove themselves to be valuable allies in the fight for human-first innovation. Ritualists do not ask for roles, they earn them. Asking for roles may result in XP loss or even banishment. Trust in the Ritual and you will see all.

Community Roles

Your journey through Ritual is marked by the community roles you earn. Each role represents a deeper level of commitment.

@Initiate

Initiate

You’re new to the community and have made it through verification. Welcome to Ritual.

@Ascendant

Ascendant

You have pledged to Ritual. This is where your true community journey begins.

@ritty bitty

ritty bitty

A little bitty baby Ritualist, recognized and on the right path, but with a long way to go. This is your first serious community role. To reach it, stay active in chat and keep posting about Ritual on X and Cura. Remember: sooner or later the team will notice you, so do not stop showing up. For most people this takes around one month. You can find more details on how to communicate and contribute in the Assets section of this site.

@ritty

ritty

Long-term, loyal community member with conviction for what Ritual is building.

This role is harder to earn. Your posts need to be high quality — quick 5‑second AI text or image generations are not enough. Recently there was a mini event in the updates channel with “mini battles”, a small contest where people could prove themselves for this role.

@Ritualist

Ritualist

The highest honor. You have authentically demonstrated your commitment to the project. With this role you enter the ranks of true server legends. It now takes a lot of time and effort: creating high‑quality content on X and Cura, staying active in chat, and showing consistent commitment. Sacrifice your time and energy to become one of them.

@Mage

Mage

A Ritualist with a mage specialization, creating writing, art, and memes that grow the community.

@Radiant Ritualist

Radiant Ritualist

Radiant Ritualist. Extremely rare and reserved for true leaders in the ecosystem. The deities of the server. Radiant Ritualists are almost unreachable: only the most dedicated, consistent and impactful community members are chosen for this role.

@Forerunner

Forerunner

Those who come from the time before Ritual and helped shape the early path.

Blessings & Curses

To bless is to curse, to curse is to bless—embrace the Ritual, for fate is woven in both. Blessings and curses both provide value in the community.

Level Up Path

XP accumulates from your messages. Check your level in #rank channel with /stats.

At level 10 you unlock your first roles:

@Ascendant

Level 10 + 10 blessings + 10 curses
Ask community for blessings/curses!

@Blessed

Level 10 + 5 blessings

@Cursed

Level 10 + 5 curses

Forever blessed, always cursed, Ritual.

/bless — give a friend a blessing.

/curse — give a friend a curse.

/stats — see your stats & level.

?confess — confess your sins in #confessions.

?sacrifice — sacrifice curses for an omen.

?oracle — spend blessings for a message from beyond.
