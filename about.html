What is Ritual?

AI + Blockchain = the next step

Ritual connects artificial intelligence (AI) with blockchain.

Before (regular AI, like ChatGPT):
Imagine you have a robot judge. It sits in a locked room in the office of one big company. You send it a question, it thinks, and makes a decision. But you can't see how it thinks. The company could secretly program it to rule in its own favor.

Now (with Ritual):
Ritual takes this robot judge out of the locked room and places it in a huge public square, surrounded by thousands of independent observers (computers all over the world).

The Essence of Ritual's Activity:
They are building a public and verifiable "brain network" for the blockchain. Any application (finance, gaming, social media) can ask the AI a question and get an answer you can be 100% confident in, because it is overseen by thousands of independent participants, and the result is recorded in a transparent digital ledger for all to see.

How it works with an example:

1. Question: Should we give this customer a discount?

2. Process: Your question is received not by one robot in a secret room, but by that very "robot in the square." Thousands of computers in the Ritual network jointly run its algorithms to compute the answer.

3. Result: They arrive at a single answer, for example, "Yes, a 10% discount."

4. The Key Part: Each computer records this conclusion on the blockchain (like in a public, tamper-proof digital ledger). Now no one can hide or change this decision. Everyone sees that the robot said "10%," and knows that no one forced it to lie.

Technologies in simple words

Infernet

A network that connects AI models (like ChatGPT or open-source models) to blockchains. It lets smart contracts call AI and get results back safely on many EVM chains.

Ritual Chain

A new blockchain designed specifically for AI workloads. It aims to make AI-native apps faster, cheaper, and more flexible.

ZK Proofs (Zero-Knowledge)

A way to prove something is true without revealing the details. For example, proving an AI followed the correct steps without showing your private data.
